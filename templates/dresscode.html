This Dress Code is for the White Wedding

Please note that traditional Nigerian attire is required for the Traditional Wedding (March 7, 2026). This dress code applies specifically to the White Wedding ceremony on April 25, 2026 in Ontario, Canada.

For Men

Tuxedos or suits with ties only. Men who want to play safe may wear a black suit with tie.

For Women

Long, elegant, colourful dresses only. Dresses should be floor-length or tea-length for formal elegance.

Not Permitted

No native or traditional attire permitted. This includes traditional fabrics, patterns, or styles.

Colour Guidelines

Formal, bright colours only. Please refer to our approved colour palette below for inspiration.
